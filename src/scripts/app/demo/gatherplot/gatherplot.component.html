Bayesian Inference - Mammogram

The absolute/relative mode of gatherplot can be used to aid the decision process of the bayesian inference problem.

The probability that a woman has breast cancer is 1%. The probability that the disease is detected by a mammography is 80%. The probability that the test detects the disease although the patient does not have it is 9.6%.

If a woman is tested as positive, what is the probability that she indeed has breast cancer?
